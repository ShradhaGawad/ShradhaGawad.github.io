Hi, I'm SG - a data thinker who finds clarity in chaos and stories in spreadsheets.

I’m a data professional who transforms complexity into clarity. With a strong foundation in Python, SQL, Excel, Power BI, and Tableau, I build tools that uncover patterns, and drive business outcomes.

My path through analytics, engineering, and quality assurance has been less about titles and more about curiosity — the kind that digs into raw data, asks better questions, and builds smarter answers. Whether I’m deep in a database or sketching out a dashboard, I bring clarity to complexity and strategy to every solution.
